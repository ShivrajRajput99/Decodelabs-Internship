## Prepared By

**Shivrajsinh Rajput**

## Project Overview

This project focuses on Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) of an e-commerce sales dataset. The objective is to analyze sales performance, identify trends, understand customer purchasing behavior, and generate business insights using data visualization and statistical analysis.

## Dataset Information

* Total Records: 1,200 Orders
* Dataset Type: E-commerce Sales Data
* Format: Excel (.xlsx)

## Objectives

* Analyze product sales performance
* Study monthly sales trends
* Examine payment method preferences
* Identify high-value transactions and outliers
* Generate business recommendations

## Tools Used

* Python
* Pandas
* NumPy
* Matplotlib
* Seaborn
* Jupyter Notebook

## Analysis Performed

### 1. Data Exploration

* Dataset structure analysis
* Data type verification
* Missing value inspection

### 2. Product Performance Analysis

* Total sales by product category
* Quantity sold analysis
* Best-selling products identification

### 3. Monthly Sales Trend Analysis

* Monthly revenue calculation
* Seasonal sales pattern identification

### 4. Payment Method Analysis

* Distribution of payment methods
* Customer payment preferences

### 5. Outlier Detection

* Identification of high-value orders
* Analysis of unusual transactions

## Key Findings

* Laptop and Printer categories generated strong sales performance.
* June recorded the highest sales revenue.
* Online payment was the most frequently used payment method.
* Several high-value transactions were identified as outliers.

## Business Recommendations

* Increase inventory during peak sales periods.
* Improve marketing efforts during low-sales months.
* Focus on high-performing product categories.
* Enhance digital payment options for customers.
